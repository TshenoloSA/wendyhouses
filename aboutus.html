About Us.



We're a proudly South African business dedicated to providing high-quality wendy houses and log cabins at affordable prices.
Our mission is to make owning a wendy house or log cabin easy, affordable, and accessible for everyone across South Africa.
Whether you're looking for extra storage space, a backyard office, a cozy playhouse, or even a larger cabin for family or guests,
we have a range of options to meet your needs.



Why Choose Us?

At Wendy Houses and Log Cabins, we believe that quality shouldn't come at a high cost.
That’s why we’re committed to offering our customers the best materials and craftsmanship at prices that fit your budget.
Our wendy houses and log cabins are built to last, crafted with strong, durable wood, and designed to withstand South African weather.
We pay close attention to every detail, from the solid construction to the fine finishes,
ensuring you get a product that is not only functional but also visually appealing.



Our Products

Affordable Rates

One of our top priorities is to keep our prices fair and affordable.
